LITERATURE

  • Essay Writing (Mal,Eng,Hin)
    • details for essay writing
    • The language of the competition shall be strictly used.
    • The topic will be given on the spot.
    • Maximum word limit is 350.
    • Entries should be in Pdf format along with the chest number.
    • Judgment will most likely be based on the qualities like the development of the theme, composition, Tone, appropriateness of style, correctness of language, originality and general impression.
    • Maximum time allowed is Two hour. Entry should be sent on time. Late entries won't be considered.
  • Short Story Writing (Mal,Eng,Hin)
    • details for short story writing
    • The story should be based on the topic and should be your own creation.
    • The story is to be completed within 2 hrs and sent to the given number.
    • The story should be in pdf format. (Name_Branch_Chessno. pdf)
    • Maximum word limit is 250.
    • All the pages should contain the exact page no and chess no. Won't be considered for evaluation otherwise.
    • Judgment will be based on qualities like delineation of character, plot construction, theme, diction, correctness, style of language and, general impression.
    • Decision of judges will be final.
  • Poem Writing (Mal,Eng,Hin)
    • details for poem writing
    • The topic will be given on the spot.
    • Maximum time allowed for writing the Poetry is two hour.Entries will not be accepted after the given time
    • Entries should be in pdf format
    • The language of the competition shall be strictly used.
    • Judgment will be based on qualities like theme development, diction, imagery, emotion, appropriate use of language, general impression etc.
    • The poem shall be original and not borrowed or copied from others.

DANCE

  • Classical Dance
    • rules for Classical Dance
    • Any form of classical dance(bharathanatyam, mohiniyattam, kuchipudi, keralanadanam)are allowed.
    • The competition will be common for both men and women.
    • No cinema songs are allowed .
    • Semiclassical forms are not allowed.
    • Judgement will be based on the qualities like tal, technique, rythm, abhinaya or expression, footwork and overall impression.
    • Costumes won't get extra points.
    • Every participant should join in the Telegram group, the link of the group will be shared through either the registered email id or phone number.
    • The chest number of each item will be provided through the Telegram group.
    • Candidate should submit the video of your performance with clear audio.
    • Candidate should conclude the performance within 6min and the perfomances with duration of more than 6 mints will be disqualified.(minimum time is 4min)
    • Candidates should pin the chest number to the costume where it is clearly visible
    • Candidate should take their chest number half an hour before event starts.
    • Prerecorded videos are not allowed.
    • Candidates should perform in front of a clear background.
    • Candidates should to make sure about their video quality. Technical issues from the your end can't be presidented..
  • Folk Dance
    • rules for Folk Dance
    • The dance can be either primitive or a folk dance but not a classical one.
    • Film folk songs are not allowed.
    • Duration of dance should not be more than 5 minute.
    • Judgement will be based on the basis of Rythm, Formation, Expressions and overall effect.
    • Video should have minimum clarity and noises should be avoided.
    • Other editings are not allowed.

MUSICAL

  • Light Music (Male/Female)
    • rules for Light Music
    • Competitions are held seperatly for men & women.
    • Only Non-films folk songs/Geetha/Gazal/Bhajan,Snabed and Abhangas cab be presented.
    • Duration of video should not be less than 2 mts & and more than 5 min
    • Candidates should pin a Chest number with good visibility.
    • Candidates should take their chest number half an hour before the event starts and also be in online.
    • Judgement will be made on the qualities like Swara,Tal,Composition,General impression.
    • Video should be a good quality and noices should be avoided.
    • Editings are not allowed.
  • Classical Music (Male/Female)
    • rules for Classical Music
    • Competitions are held seperatly for men & women.
    • Item can be presented either in Hindustani or Karnatik style.
    • Maximum time permitted for the item is ten (10) minutes.
    • Cinema songs are not allowed under this item.
    • Sufficient thought and care must be exercised in the choice of raga and composition.( No sound modulation allowed).
    • Judgment will be based on the qualities like Swara, Tal, selection of Raga, composition and general impression.
    • Competitions are held separately for men and women participants
  • Western Vocal
    • rules for Western Vocal
    • The competition is common to men and women participants
    • The No. of accompanists allowed is one.
    • Time allotted for item is not more than 5 minutes.
    • Language of the song shall be only English.
    • Judgment will be made on the qualities like composition, rhythm, co- ordination and general impression.
    • Video should have minimum clarity and noices should be avoided.
    • Other editing are not allowed.
  • Mappilappattu
    • rules for Mappilappattu
    • The competition is common to both men and women participants.
    • Candidates should record a video of your performance with the alloted chess number and pre-recorded videos are not allowed.
    • Maximum alloted time for this event is 3 minutes.
    • Evaluation will be based on sruthilayam and rythm, bhavam, language and literacy clarity.
    • Film songs are not allowed for the competition.
    • Entries will not be accepted after the given extra time.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
